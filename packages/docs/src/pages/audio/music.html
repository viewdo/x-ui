Music

This page has background music configured. You should start to hear music after a moment.

Add music to liven up your content!

<x-app-view-do> routes support background music.

Load Music

Just include a <x-audio-music-load> element in the content and it will play as soon as it is loaded.

Add Controls, or don't!

You can make any button control the audio, using the <x-action-activator> with the <x-audio-music-action> components.
